About Me

Who I Am & What I Build

I'm a Full Stack Developer based in Nepal with 2.5+ years of professional experience at Gateway Technology Pvt. Ltd. — building enterprise web applications using ASP.NET Core, ASP.NET MVC, C#, SQL Server, and MySQL.

My biggest professional highlight is contributing to the Market Monitoring System — a live government platform for Nepal's Department of Commerce tracking commodity prices nationally.

I've independently architected School, Library, Finance, and Academic management systems, then migrated all three original MVC codebases into a unified ASP.NET Core application. I also built a fully-featured SaaS Consultancy Referral System with RBAC, role-based menu access, activity tracking, and hospital referral workflows.

On the side I freelance in WordPress development and carry UI/UX design skills from my internship at Cubix Tech.
